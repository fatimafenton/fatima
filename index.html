Diabetes

What is diabetes

Diabetes is a defect in the body's ability to convert glucose(sugar)to energy.Glucose is the main source of fuel for our body.
When food is disgested it is changed into fats,protein,or carbohyrates.Foods that affect blood sugars are called carbohydrates.
when digested it is changed into glucose(examples of carbs bread,rice,paste,potatoes etc)
Glucose is then transferred to the blood and is used by the cells for energy.
In order for glucose to be transferred from the blood into the cells,the hormane-insulin is needed.
Insulin is produced by the beta cells in the pancreas(the organ that produces insulin)
